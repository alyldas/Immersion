We partner with schools globally to develop transformative entrepreneurship programs.

In addition to teaching students ourselves, we partner with many schools all over the world to deliver turnkey classes on Design Thinking.

Most schools are looking to modernise their curriculums by integrating entrepreneurship and social-emotional learning. In particular, schools are embracing their responsibility as stewards and increasing engagement between their students and the wider community.

At Immersion, we work with our partner schools to deliver classes on entrepreneurship, creativity, and social impact. Our classes are all inspired by Stanford curricula and have seen a dramatic increase in engagement and extracurricular attainment in students who have taken them. Having tangible, project-based classes in which students solve problems brings about other benefits: deeper inter-generational empathy between students and teachers, deepened alumni relations, and effective teacher training.

  • A Personalised Curriculum
  • Ready-made Teaching Presentation
  • Teacher Notes and Training
  • Superior Quality at a Competitive Price
