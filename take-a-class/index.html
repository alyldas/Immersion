IMMERSION

Immersion classes are a truly unique, entrepreneurial, and empowering learning experience.

Our Purpose.

To tell you that our classes are not like any other would simply not do them justice. At Immersion, our goal is to blow our students away, each and every time.

By combining wonderful visuals with a wide range of activities and relaxed teaching style, students never quite feel like they’re in “school”. That’s because they’re not. Our courses develop students beyond a traditional class and instil in them the infectious self-belief you see in great entrepreneurs. All of our classes are adapted from Stanford curricula and give students an understanding of:

Navigate Ambiguity

How to solve complex, ambiguous problems

LEARN FROM OTHERS

How to be an empathetic listener

Experiment Rapidly

How to rapidly build out and test your ideas to make more informed decisions

Think Collaboratively

How to lead teams effectively and solve organizational problems

Design Purposefully

How to build delightful products and lasting companies
